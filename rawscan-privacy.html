RawScan Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

Overview

RawScan ("we", "our", or "us") is developed by Kobe Studio. This Privacy Policy explains how RawScan handles information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Do Not Collect

RawScan does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any contact information
  • We do not access your photos or camera roll beyond what you explicitly scan within the app
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools
  • We do not display advertisements
  • We do not share any data with third parties

Camera Usage

RawScan requests access to your device's camera solely for the purpose of scanning and photographing documents. Images captured are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted to external servers.

Local Storage

All scanned documents are saved locally on your device. We have no access to these files. If you delete the app, all locally stored data will be removed.

Children's Privacy

RawScan does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
